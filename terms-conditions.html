2. Refunds

Once the project is booked, you are provided with a 72-hour free look window during which you may choose to cancel the project. After this window, no refunds will be provided if the project is cancelled.

3. Booking Fee

Your booking fee (10% payment) is adjusted against future orders. If the scope of your project increases significantly, you shall be required to pay 10% for the new scope before design can begin.

4. Execution Stages

Execution stages help us track the progress of your order. They mark the completion of an important activity at which balance payment (for that order type) will be due. Eesha Design Studio can proceed with completing the rest of the order only after the amount due for that execution stage has been paid.

  • Proposal prices are valid for 21 days.
  • Prices may vary if the booking is done after this period or if there are changes in scope or requirements.
